Olympus II

Olympus II embodies the next-phase spirit of Project Olympus, the Mars-focused program Jared Isaacman detailed in his Project Athena blueprint. It underscores preparations for uncrewed Mars infrastructure landings, nuclear propulsion advancements, and collaborative pathways to enable American astronauts on Mars in the coming decades.

Strong domain for branding around the groundbreaking Mars agenda from the Athena document, emphasizing speed, innovation, and the journey to human Mars exploration.
